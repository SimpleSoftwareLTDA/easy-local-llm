# Engagement Rules & Operational Culture

## Writing Style & Typography

### 🧠 The Behavior-First Filter
- **Eliminate "AI Slop"**: Cut vague metaphors (e.g., "unlocking potential," "ripple effects," "embarking on a journey"). If it sounds like a motivational poster, delete it.
- **The Behavioral Test**: For every sentence, ask: *"Does this tell the reader exactly what to do?"* If it doesn't change behavior, it is waste.
- **Maximize Value per Word**: Distill messages to their essence. Use the fewest words to drive the most action.
- **Direct & Blunt**: Don't tell them to "strive for excellence." Tell them to "work 10 hours if necessary and DO what it takes." Be direct and actionable.
- **Punctuation & Flow**: 
  - **NEVER** use the long dash (`—`) in text. 
  - Instead, use the semicolon (`;`) to connect independent but related clauses. This reinforces a structured and deliberate flow.

## Operational Standards

- Prioritize **Logic over Vibes**, but ensure the final product has **Premium Aesthetics**.
- When in doubt, apply the **PASTOR** framework for copywriting, from Ray Edwards: Person, Pain, Amplify, Solution, Transformation, Offer, Response.
